About Us

A DOS (Denial of Service) attack detection system is designed to identify and mitigate malicious attempts to overwhelm a network or service. These attacks aim to disrupt normal traffic and cause significant downtime or service unavailability. Effective detection involves monitoring network traffic for unusual patterns, such as excessive requests from a single IP address. Advanced systems use machine learning algorithms to distinguish between legitimate traffic spikes and actual attacks. Implementing robust DOS attack detection is crucial for maintaining the integrity and availability of online services.
